To the Queen of Happiness

Oh, those eyes of yours! Pure innocence wrapped in a sparkle that could distract even the most serious of souls. And that smile? It’s like you’ve found the secret to happiness in a butterfly flitting by or a leaf falling from a tree. Seriously, it’s impressive how easily you get excited over the little things—like you’re on a never-ending quest for joy in the mundane. It’s almost like you’re a walking reminder to us all that life isn’t as serious as we make it. Keep spreading that whimsical energy; the world needs more butterflies like you!
